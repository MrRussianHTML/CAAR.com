The Rescue

Located in northern Mississippi, we are a family owned animal rescue. Although we are an "animal rescue" we primarily work with equine (horses, donkies, mules, ect). After 15+ years of hounorable service in the United Sates Army, the companies founder Samantha Miller decided she wasn't done helping those in need, So she opened the rescue to animals who were mistreated, mal neurished, unloved, or abused and is making an attempt at giving them a better chance at a normal life worth living.
